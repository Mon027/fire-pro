About Systems Voltage Construction

Systems Voltage Construction is a trusted contractor providing reliable, safe, and cost-effective solutions for fire protection across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

We specialize in comprehensive fire protection solutions, including fire alarm and suppression systems, sprinklers, emergency lighting, exit signs, inspections, and custom fire safety design and consultation.

Our Fire Protection Services

Fire Alarm Systems

Design, installation, and maintenance of comprehensive fire alarm systems including smoke detectors, heat sensors, and notification devices for early fire detection.

Sprinkler Systems

Complete fire sprinkler system installation including wet, dry, and deluge systems for optimal fire suppression in all building types.

Fire Suppression Systems

Specialized fire suppression systems including clean agent, foam, and CO2 systems for critical facilities and equipment protection.

Emergency Lighting & Exit Signs

Installation and maintenance of emergency lighting systems and illuminated exit signs to ensure safe evacuation during emergencies.

Fire Safety Inspections

Regular fire safety inspections, testing, and maintenance services to ensure all systems remain operational and compliant with codes.

Fire Safety Design & Consultation
